🌳 Tree of Life

Each band is a species — a cluster of genetically similar creatures — stacked by abundance over time. Watch lineages appear, sweep to dominance, and go extinct. Click one to spotlight it in the pond above.

No creature is programmed to seek food. Each one just runs a small neural network it inherited; the ones whose networks happen to steer them toward food live long enough to reproduce. Everything that looks like purpose is natural selection at work. Built by Claude — see the devlog.
